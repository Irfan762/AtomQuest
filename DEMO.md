# GoalGrid AI-Powered Demo Walkthrough

Follow these steps to wow the judges with the advanced AI and enterprise features.

### 1. The Executive Command Center (Leadership View)
- Login as `admin@company.com` (use the **Demo Switcher** at the bottom left).
- Navigate to **Executive Insights**.
- **Showcase**: The Organization Heatmap showing intensity across departments.
- **Showcase**: The **Risk Index**. Point out goals that the AI predicts will fail.
- **Showcase**: The Leaderboard with "Goal Master" and "KPI Crusher" badges.

### 2. AI SMART Goal Setting (Employee View)
- Switch to `alice@company.com`.
- Go to **My Goals** -> **Create Goal**.
- Type a vague goal: "Do better sales".
- Click **Analyze Goal** in the AI Assistant panel.
- **Showcase**: The **SMART Score** and suggestions.
- Click **Apply AI Optimization** to see the goal transform into a professional KPI.

### 3. AI Performance Review (Manager View)
- Switch to `manager@company.com`.
- Go to **Team Performance**.
- Select Alice Adams from the list.
- Click the **Generate AI Review** button.
- **Showcase**: The AI-generated summary, strengths, and areas for improvement based on her real data.

### 4. Interactive AI Chatbot
- Open the **Chatbot** (bottom right).
- Ask: "Who is the top performer?"
- Ask: "Show delayed goals".
- **Showcase**: The bot's ability to analyze data and provide navigation links to relevant pages.

### 5. Gamification & Engagement
- On the **Dashboard**, show the **12-Day Streak** flame.
- Hover over the **Achievement Badges** to show the requirements for "Q2 Warrior".
- Demonstrate how notifications (Bell icon) show real-time "Teams-style" approval alerts.

node seedEnterprise.js
